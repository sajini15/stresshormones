Level 1: Overview of Hormones

Welcome to Level 1! In this level, we will dive into the fascinating world of hormones. Learn how they influence various processes in the body!

1. Cortisol

Type: Steroid hormone

Production Site: Adrenal cortex, specifically the zona fasciculata layer

Trigger for Release: Stress and low blood glucose levels, regulated by ACTH

Target Organs/Tissues:

  • Liver: Stimulates gluconeogenesis
  • Muscles: Protein catabolism
  • Adipose Tissue: Lipolysis
  • Immune System: Immunosuppression

2. Corticotrophin-Releasing Hormone (CRH)

Type: Peptide hormone

Production Site: Hypothalamus in the brain

Trigger for Release: Stress signals from the central nervous system

Target Organs/Tissues:

  • Stimulates the anterior pituitary gland to secrete ACTH

3. Adrenocorticotropic Hormone (ACTH)

Type: Peptide hormone

Production Site: Anterior pituitary gland

Trigger for Release: CRH from the hypothalamus

Target Organs/Tissues:

  • Stimulates the adrenal cortex to release cortisol
